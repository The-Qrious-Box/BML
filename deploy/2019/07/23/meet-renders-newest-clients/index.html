.Meet Render’s Newest Clients!

Get to know the newest gamers we have the pleasure of making content for this year! 

FNATIC – REKKLES & BROXAH

We started working for Fnatic in January 2019 and currently produce edited highlight videos showcasing the best moments from both players’ live Twitch streams. Render uploads these videos on their personal YouTube channels along with creating titles and thumbnails to ensure maximum clickthrough and viewership potential. We’ve also edited videos for Fnatic’s main team YouTube channel which includes highlights from their pro LoL matches and a team Fortnite montage.

Rekkles plays as Fnatic’s ADC and Broxah is their starting Jungler. Both put aside time to live stream games to grow their personal brand, and we’re thrilled to be helping them get to the next level!

STREAM ELEMENTS – LOEYA 

We approached Stream Elements to partner with Render in May 2019 which lead to working with Loeya, a streamer who uses their live streaming software to enable her twitch streams. We provided a full service offering to Loeya, which includes 4 videos a week and overseeing YouTube channel management that encompasses thumbnail creation, titling and SEO tagging, ad enablement, etc.

Loeya is a live streamer who focuses on publishing personality-driven content through her Fortnite gameplay. She has a unique approach to streaming, committing almost every day to it. She’s often live for an average of 10 hours! That’s dedication. 

MMG

MMG (Madden Mobile Gods) is best known for his gameplay commentary-style content. He’s currently focused on producing NBA 2K videos for his 1,000,000+ YouTube subscribers. We now assist him by producing 3 videos a week for his channel. With our editors on the scene, he can now spend more time doing what he loves: ideating on new content pieces! 

OH LONG JOHNSON

Oh Long Johnson is a Fortnite highlight publisher who boasts over 2,600,000 YouTube subscribers. His videos bring a unique twist to a more traditional highlight channel, incorporating highly engaging edits and memes. Render gathers clips and produces edited highlights to increase overall video output on his channel. This way more time can be put into content generation while we tackle the production.
